Subject: [Project Name] - Clarification Needed on Contract Scope

---

Hi [Contact Name],

Thank you for sending over the contract for [Project Name].

Before we can proceed, we need clarification on the following:

**Scope Questions:**
- [Question 1 - e.g., "The contract shows X but our estimate included Y. Can you confirm which is correct?"]
- [Question 2]
- [Question 3]

**Missing Information:**
- [Item 1 - e.g., "We need the Schedule of Values to set up billing"]
- [Item 2 - e.g., "Please confirm the site superintendent contact"]
- [Item 3]

**Variance:**
- Our estimate: $[Amount]
- Contract value: $[Amount]
- Difference: $[Amount]

[If applicable: "The difference appears to be due to [reason]. Please confirm this is correct."]

Once we have this information, we can finalize the contract and get started.

Please let me know if you have any questions.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
