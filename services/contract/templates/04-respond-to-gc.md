# Response Actions

Standard actions to complete after contract extraction and reconciliation. These are things we DO, not things we extract.

---

## Always Required

These happen on basically every project:

### Certificate of Insurance (COI)

- [ ] Request COI from WTW (Katie Beck)
- [ ] Include correct certificate holder name and address
- [ ] Include additional insured parties (from contract)
- [ ] Specify endorsements needed:
  - [ ] Additional Insured - ongoing operations
  - [ ] Additional Insured - completed operations
  - [ ] Primary and Non-Contributory
  - [ ] Waiver of Subrogation
- [ ] Include project name/number on certificate
- [ ] Confirm limits match contract requirements (see 03-check-insurance.md)
